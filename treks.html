Our Treks

Poon Hill Trek

The Poon Hill Trek is also known as the Annapurna Sunrise Trek this 4 day trek will take you to the Summit of Poon Hill (3,210m) where you can see the sunrise over the Annapurna Range, one of the most beautiful mountain ranges in the world. This is one of Nepals most scenic and also easiest treks, you won’t have to worry about altitude sickness, and you can do it in as little as 4 days, a perfect introduction to trekking in Nepal

from £199.99

Mardi Himal Trek

Mardi Himal is a challenging and beautiful 4-day trek in the Annapurna region that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan mountain range. It’s a lesser-known route compared to the famous Everest Base Camp trek or Annapurna Circuit trek. It takes you up to the Mardi Himal Base Camp at 4,500m and can be combined with many other treks.

from £399.99
