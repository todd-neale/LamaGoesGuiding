Mardi Himal Trek

Mardi Himal is a challenging and beautiful 4-day trek in the Annapurna region that offers breathtaking views of the Himalayan mountain range. It’s a lesser-known route compared to the famous Everest Base Camp trek or Annapurna Circuit trek. It takes you up to the Mardi Himal Base Camp at 4,500m and can be combined with many other treks.

About

Many assume walking in the Himalayas is only for rugged types who enjoy roughing it. That was true in 1953, when Everest was first climbed and trekking tourism didn’t exist. Now there are new ways to experience the Himalayas: luxury lodges for those looking to take in the views with a bit of comfort; treks that focus as much on culture as scenery; and new lodges and homestays for those who want to relax and get beneath the surface of Himalayan life.


When is the best time to visit Nepal?

The dry season in Nepal runs from October through to May. October/November and March/April/May are the most popular times to travel as the weather is typically warm and sunny. The clear skies during this period also afford fabulous mountain views.

December to February the daytime temperatures drop a little but nights may be especially cold. At higher altitudes temperatures can drop significantly. The monsoon falls on Nepal from June to September. The climate is humid and wet during this time.
